Why Study MBBS in Russia?

Russia has been a top destination for Indian students for decades due to its high educational standards and affordable lifestyle.

  • Affordable Tuition: Fees start as low as $3,000/year, much cheaper than private Indian colleges.
  • Global Recognition: Degrees recognized by WHO, NMC (India), and major medical councils worldwide.
  • English Medium: Most universities offer MBBS entirely in English.
  • FMGE Screening: High success rate for Indian students returning to practice.
