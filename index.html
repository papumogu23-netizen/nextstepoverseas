Why Study at FEFU?

Far Eastern Federal University (FEFU) is one of Russia's top-rated federal universities. Located in the beautiful coastal city of Vladivostok, it offers a modern campus and a globally recognized MBBS degree.

  • English Medium: Complete course taught in English.
  • Duration: 6 Years (5 Years Study + 1 Year Internship).
  • Recognition: NMC (India), WHO, & UNESCO approved.
  • Indian Mess: Availability of Indian food on campus.
